## Q2 2026 (Apr-Jun)

### Goals
- Launch notification system MVP (Q2 target)
- Reduce onboarding drop-off by 15% (measured via analytics)
- Establish user research cadence (bi-weekly interviews)
- Ship 3 iterations of the dashboard redesign

### Key Achievements
Significant accomplishments. Use STAR format: **[What you did]**: Situation - Action - Result.

- **Led cross-functional launch of in-app notifications**: Users were missing critical updates (payment failures, team invites) because we only had email - 23% open rate. Ran 8 user interviews to validate the gap, wrote the PRD, coordinated eng/design/marketing, managed 3 beta rounds with 200 users, iterated on frequency controls after beta feedback showed alert fatigue - Launched to 100% of users. In-app engagement 4x email (89% vs 23%). Reduced support tickets for "missed payment" by 34%.

- **Redesigned onboarding flow based on user research**: New user activation was 41%, below the 55% target. Analyzed drop-off funnel, identified 3 friction points (account setup, team creation, first project). Ran 12 user interviews, created journey map, proposed progressive disclosure approach. A/B tested 3 variants over 4 weeks - Activation improved to 58% (+17pp). Time-to-first-value reduced from 12 min to 4 min.

- **Established bi-weekly user research program**: Product decisions were based on stakeholder opinions rather than user data. Proposed and set up recurring research sessions - recruited panel of 30 users across segments, created interview guide templates, built insight repository in Notion - 24 interviews conducted in Q2. Three major product decisions changed direction based on findings.
